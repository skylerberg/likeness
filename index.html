    How to play
    
        
    Travel from the Start word to the Target word, one word at a time.
    
        
      
          
    • ➕ Add a letter anywhere in the previous word. (1 step)
      • 
          
    • ➖ Delete a letter anywhere in the previous word. (1 step)
      • 
          
    • 🔁 Replace a letter — swap any one letter for another. (1 step)
      • 
          
    • 🔄 Synonym — swap to a word that means the same thing. (free)
      • 
          
    • 🔊 Homophone — swap to a word that sounds the same. (free)
      • 
        
    
        
    When you reach the target, share your chain — and the next player picks up where you left off.
    
        
    Synonym and homophone judgements come from the Datamuse word API and may be liberal: if it groups them, the game accepts them.
